What is Foundry Companion App?

The Foundry Companion App is a mobile application designed to enhance your D&D5e experience with Foundry VTT while keeping the load on your device minimal.

It provides an optimized interface for accessing your character sheets, spells, and items on your phone or tablet, allowing you to stay engaged in the game without needing to switch to a desktop interface.

It relies on a Foundry VTT module, the Companion Bridge, to connect to your game and provide real-time updates to your character sheets, and interations with your spells and items.

Why Use the Foundry Companion App?

IRL Games

  • Perfect for people playing around a table but still using Foundry VTT.
  • No need to try and fit several laptops around the game table anymore.
  • No risk of forgetting your character sheet at home or losing it.

Online Games

  • Integrated for seamless interactions with Foundry VTT system.
  • Keep your screen dedicated to the game, the map, the artworks, etc., while still having access to your character information.
  • Roll everything needed directly from your mobile device and see the results immediately in the game.

How to use the app?

How does the Foundry Companion App work?

Foundry Companion app works by combining two softwares:

Companion Bridge Module

Free / Premium Versions

The Companion Bridge module is installed on your Foundry VTT server. It acts as a bridge between the server and the mobile app, enabling real-time synchronization.

The module is available in both free and premium versions, offering different levels of functionality and integration with Foundry VTT.

Companion App

Free Access

How do I get access?

Memberships

The Foundry Companion app is free to download and access to the some app feautures are dependent on your membership tier.

Membership tiers include:

Free Tier

Copper Tier: Scout
This tier is for players who want to try out the module and the app. Experience a clean, real-time window into the game, no more laptops blocking the view.

  • Real-Time Read Sync: All connected players can view their stats, inventory, and spells as they change in Foundry.
  • Local Updates: Players can change the local state of their character (HP, spell slots, exhaustion, etc...)
  • Patreon Updates: Receive the latest news and updates about the app and module from Patreon!
  • Limitations: Does not include the possibility to send changes to Foundry, cast spells, use items, etc.
