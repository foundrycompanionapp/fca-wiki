Foundry Companion App: Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Foundry Companion App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.
This policy explains how we handle data when you use our mobile application and our associated Foundry VTT module.

Our guiding principle is Data Minimization: we only process the data strictly necessary to provide a functional character sheet experience.

2. Data Collection

The Foundry Companion App is a client-side tool.

  • Character Data:
    All character stats, spells, and items are stored on your own self-hosted Foundry VTT server or your GM’s server.
  • No Data Storage:
    We do not own, store, or have access to your character data. The connection is a direct link between your mobile device and the Foundry VTT server URL you provide.

3. Data We Collect and How We Use It

  • Local Storage:
    We use Capacitor Preferences (local storage) on your device to remember your Server URL, Actor ID, and UI preferences (like Pinned Tabs). This data stays on your device.
  • Authentication Data:
    To connect to Foundry, you enter a username and password. This is sent directly to your Foundry server via a secure socket. We never see or store your passwords.
  • Patreon Integration:
    If you use the premium version of the module, Foundry VTT verifies your subscription status via the Patreon API. We do not receive your Patreon information, nor credit card or billing details.
  • Tally.so:
    We collect names and email addresses via Tally.so for the sole purpose of distributing beta testing invites and project updates. This data is never shared or sold. You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Patreon:
    Used for subscription verification. Patreon Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services / Apple App Store:
    Used for app distribution and (eventual) processing in-app purchases.
  • Socket.io:
    Used for the technical real-time connection between the app and the server.
  • Foundry VTT:
    Used for the core functionality of the app, which connects to your Foundry VTT server.

5. Legal Compliance (Regional)

A. European Union (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data we may hold. Since we do not store your character data on our own servers, you should contact your Game Master or server host to exercise these rights regarding your character data.

B. California, USA (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), you have the right to know what personal information we collect about you, the right to delete that information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Since we do not store your character data on our own servers, you should contact your Game Master or server host to exercise these rights regarding your character data.

C. Asian Markets (APPI, PDPA, PIPL)

Under the Asian data protection laws (APPI, PDPA, PIPL), you have the right to know what personal information we collect about you, the right to delete that information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Since we do not store your character data on our own servers, you should contact your Game Master or server host to exercise these rights regarding your character data.

7. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

The Foundry Companion App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Account and Data Deletion

  • In-App Data:
    You can clear all app data at any time by clearing the app cache in your device settings or using the "Change Server" button to wipe local session data.
  • Subscription Data:
    You can manage or cancel your data through your Patreon account settings.

9. Future Monetization

While the app is currently free to download, future versions may include In-App Purchases. Any such transactions will be handled securely by Apple or Google; we will never store your financial information on our systems.
